Zach Rice

blog · resume · github

Zach is a computer programmer currently working at Gannett (USATODAY Network). Prior to Gannett, he worked for an agricultural-tech start-up, Agrible, that had a succesful exit and was bought by Nutrien. Before that he recieved his Bachelor's from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in Computer Engineering and held a position as a TA for ECE 220, an introduction to C programming course. He is a lover of open source and the creator of gitleaks, a popular open source security tool for auditing git repos for secrets. In his free time Zach enjoys reading fiction, drawing, working on his bicycle, making music, playing with his cat and family dog, and cooking. He currently lives in Urbana, IL, which is roughly 4/9 of Urbana-Champaign. Although he lives in Urbana currently, he considers himself a Champaignite as he spent his childhood and adolecense in Champaign.
